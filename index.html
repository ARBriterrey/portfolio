About Me

I am Andhan Rahul Buddhan, an aspiring researcher and developer specializing in Deep Learning, Machine Learning, and Cybersecurity. I hold an Integrated Master's from IISER Thiruvananthapuram, where I worked on AI-driven cybersecurity solutions and computational modeling. My research includes ML and DL-based threat detection, as well as optical fiber frequency shifts, combining mathematical modeling, programming (Python, Java, C++), and cloud computing. With publications on AI and Augmented Reality, I am passionate about interdisciplinary research, TinyML, Edge Devices, and IoT, aiming to bridge the gap between AI and real-world applications.
